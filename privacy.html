Dose Pop

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

The short version: Dose Pop is local-first. We do not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal data.

Data we collect

None. Dose Pop does not collect personal data and does not use advertising or tracking services.

Data stored on your device

Dose Pop stores your medication list, dose history, notification preferences, and app settings locally on your device using Apple frameworks.

iCloud sync

If iCloud sync is enabled by Apple system settings, app data may sync through your own iCloud account. The developer does not have access to your iCloud data.

Medication search

When you search for a medication name, Dose Pop may make anonymous, read-only requests to public medication databases such as RxNorm and OpenFDA. These requests contain the search text you type and do not include an account, advertising identifier, or tracking identifier.

Notifications

Dose Pop uses Apple local notifications to remind you when it is time to take a medication. Notification content is scheduled and shown by your device and does not pass through a Dose Pop server.

Caregiver alerts

Caregiver cloud alerts are not active in this local-first version. If a future version adds caregiver alerts or cloud sharing, this policy and the App Store privacy answers will be updated before that feature is released.

Children

Dose Pop is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from any user.

Medical disclaimer

Dose Pop is a reminder tool. It does not diagnose, treat, prescribe, or provide medical advice. Always follow instructions from your doctor, pharmacist, or other qualified healthcare professional.
