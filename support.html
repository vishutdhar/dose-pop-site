Support

Simple medication reminders for iPhone and iPad.

Need help with Dose Pop? Email support@freedom-terminal.com and we'll get back to you.

Common checks

  • Open iPhone Settings → Notifications → Dose Pop and confirm notifications are allowed.
  • Check Focus modes and Silent Mode if reminders are not making sound.
  • Open Dose Pop after changing medication times so reminders can be refreshed.
  • Confirm the medication name, dose, and scheduled time are entered correctly.
